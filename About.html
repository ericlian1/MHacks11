The New Era of Stock Trading

Market Prediction using Machine Learning



Our project aims to provide investors with valuable insights on the major stocks in the market. By using the Marquee API provided by Goldman Sachs, we were able to extract key atrributes on the stocks, and combine them with historic stock prices to train a neural network on Google Cloud to predict future stock prices, growth, financial returns, multiple and integrated factor.



To further provide insights to the investors, we collected news articles from authorative sources such as Bloomberg, along with tweets, to implement sentiment analysis and return a positive or negative tone on the company of interest. The investors are also able to look through the tweets and articles.
